# ADR Numbering Policy

Format:

ADR-001
ADR-002
ADR-003

---

Rules

Numbers are sequential.

Numbers are never reused.

Deleted ADRs are prohibited.

---

Examples

ADR-001-MICROSERVICES.md

ADR-002-KAFKA.md

ADR-003-KUBERNETES.md

ADR-004-REDIS.md

ADR-005-POSTGRESQL.md

---

Reserved Ranges

001-099

Core Platform

100-199

Security

200-299

Infrastructure

300-399

Data Platform

400-499

ML Platform
