# ADR Lifecycle

## Proposed

ADR created.

Not yet reviewed.

---

## Under Review

ARB evaluating proposal.

---

## Approved

Implementation allowed.

---

## Rejected

Proposal denied.

Implementation prohibited.

---

## Deprecated

ADR no longer recommended.

---

## Superseded

Replaced by newer ADR.

Example:

ADR-021 supersedes ADR-005.
