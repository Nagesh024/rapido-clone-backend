# Emergency Rollback Procedure

## Trigger Conditions

Error Rate > 5%

Latency Increase > 50%

Payment Failure

Authentication Failure

Ride Booking Failure

---

## Actions

1. Stop Green Traffic

2. Redirect Traffic To Blue

3. Verify Health Checks

4. Validate Business Flows

5. Notify Stakeholders

---

## Target Recovery Time

Less Than 5 Minutes
