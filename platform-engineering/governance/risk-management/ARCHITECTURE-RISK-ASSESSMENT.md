# Architecture Risk Assessment Framework

## Risk Categories

- Scalability Risk
- Security Risk
- Operational Risk
- Financial Risk
- Compliance Risk

## Risk Formula

Risk Score = Impact × Likelihood

## Severity Matrix

1-5       LOW

6-10      MEDIUM

11-15     HIGH

16-25     CRITICAL

## Review Frequency

Critical  → Weekly

High      → Bi-Weekly

Medium    → Monthly

Low       → Quarterly
