# Risk Management Policy

## Purpose

Identify, assess, track, mitigate, and monitor risks across the platform.

---

## Risk Categories

- Security
- Reliability
- Availability
- Compliance
- Scalability
- Operational
- Financial

---

## Risk Review Frequency

Monthly

---

## Risk Owners

Every risk must have an assigned owner.

---

## Escalation

Critical risks must be reported to the Architecture Review Board immediately.
