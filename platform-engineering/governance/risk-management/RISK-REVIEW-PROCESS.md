# Risk Review Process

## Monthly Review

Conducted by Architecture Review Board.

---

## Inputs

- Security scans
- Incident reports
- Technical debt reviews
- Compliance audits
- Dependency reviews

---

## Outputs

- New risks identified
- Existing risks updated
- Mitigation plans approved
- Risks closed

---

## Escalation Rules

Critical Risk:
Immediate review

High Risk:
Review within 7 days

Medium Risk:
Review monthly

Low Risk:
Monitor
