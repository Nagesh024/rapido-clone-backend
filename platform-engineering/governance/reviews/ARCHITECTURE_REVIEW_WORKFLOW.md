# Architecture Review Workflow

## Workflow

Developer Proposal
        ↓
Architecture Review
        ↓
Risk Assessment
        ↓
Approval / Rejection
        ↓
Implementation

---

## Stage 1

Developer Proposal

Owner:
Engineering Team

Deliverables:

- Architecture Proposal
- Impact Analysis
- ADR Draft
- Cost Estimate

Output:

Review Request

---

## Stage 2

Architecture Review

Owner:
Architecture Review Board

Validation:

- Design Quality
- Service Boundaries
- Scalability
- Reliability

Output:

Review Findings

---

## Stage 3

Risk Assessment

Owner:
Security Lead
Chief Architect

Validation:

- Security Risk
- Compliance Risk
- Operational Risk
- Financial Risk

Output:

Risk Score

---

## Stage 4

Approval Decision

Possible Outcomes:

Approved

Approved With Conditions

Rejected

Escalated

Output:

Decision Record

---

## Stage 5

Implementation

Owner:
Engineering Team

Requirements:

- ADR Approved
- Risks Mitigated
- Compliance Passed

Output:

Implementation Completed
