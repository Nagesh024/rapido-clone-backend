# Technical Debt Review Process

## Monthly Review

The Architecture Review Board reviews all open technical debt items monthly.

---

## Review Inputs

- SonarQube findings
- Security scan findings
- Dependency scan findings
- Incident postmortems
- Architecture reviews

---

## Review Outputs

- Debt accepted
- Debt scheduled
- Debt escalated
- Debt resolved

---

## Escalation Rules

Critical Debt:
Fix immediately.

High Debt:
Fix within next release.

Medium Debt:
Add to backlog.

Low Debt:
Track and monitor.
