# Engineering KPI Framework

## DORA Metrics

### Deployment Frequency
Number of deployments per week

### Change Failure Rate
Percentage of failed releases

### Lead Time
Time from commit to production

### MTTR
Mean Time To Recovery

### Availability
Service uptime percentage

## Targets

Deployment Frequency:
> 10 per week

Change Failure Rate:
< 15%

Lead Time:
< 24 hours

MTTR:
< 1 hour

Availability:
> 99.9%
